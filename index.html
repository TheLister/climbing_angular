About

This is the official website for Refuge Bouldering: A Bouldering Guidebook to the Wichita Mountains. Check in weekly for new videos showcasing new development in the Refuge. The Open Projects tab will also highlight current open projects inside the Refuge. Tentative release date for the book is Fall 2020, but stay tuned for updates!
